Legal & Compliance

history Last updated: October 24, 2023

Transparency and security are at the core of our offline payment technology. This document outlines how we handle your data, the terms of using our offline capabilities, and your rights as a user.

01. Privacy Policy

At Offline UPI, we take your privacy seriously. We collect minimal data necessary to facilitate secure peer-to-peer transactions without an active internet connection.

  • check_box_outline_blank Transaction Logs: We store a local encrypted log of your last 50 transactions on your device.
  • check_box_outline_blank Device Identity: We use a unique device fingerprint to authorize offline tokens.
  • check_box_outline_blank Audio/NFC Data: Proximity data is processed transiently and is never stored on our servers.
wifi_lock

02. Offline Storage Behavior

Our unique "Store & Sync" architecture ensures you can pay anywhere. Here is specifically how data behaves when your device is disconnected:

hourglass_top Local Queuing

Transactions are cryptographically signed and queued in a secure enclave on your device storage.

sync_alt Auto-Sync

Once connectivity is restored (Wi-Fi or Cellular), the app automatically pushes the queue to the banking ledger.

03. Terms of Service

By downloading or using the app, these terms will automatically apply to you – you should make sure therefore that you read them carefully before using the app.

Liability Limitations

Offline UPI is not liable for transaction failures caused by hardware malfunction of the user's device. While we ensure 99.9% sync accuracy, the user is responsible for maintaining device security (e.g., biometric locks).

Merchant Agreements

Merchants accepting Offline UPI payments agree to honor the digital receipt generated at the point of sale, regardless of immediate settlement status.

04. User Rights

You retain full ownership of your financial data. In compliance with global data protection standards, you have the right to:

Right to Access

Request a full copy of your transaction history including offline logs.

Right to Delete

Request complete deletion of your account and associated cloud data.
