Security & Privacy

Trust by Protocol.

Built on NPCI *99# USSD Infrastructure

01. The *99# Standard

OfflinePay leverages the National Unified USSD Platform (NUUP). By using native cellular signaling instead of the internet, your payments work even when 4G/5G fails.

02. SIM-Linked Security

Every transaction is verified through your encrypted SIM signature. No internet means no packet-sniffing or Wi-Fi vulnerabilities—purely hardware-level trust.

03. Zero Data Footprint

Since the USSD channel bypasses the mobile data layer entirely, your transaction metadata is never exposed to third-party apps or malicious trackers.
